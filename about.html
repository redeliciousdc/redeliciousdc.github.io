What is ReDelicious?

We’re a worker cooperative of local foragers, mushroom growers and food lovers founded in 2022. Each week we divert and redistribute food that would otherwise go to waste from farmers markets and local businesses.

We invest in a kitchen lab and culture library to learn about food preservation, fermentation and ecology while building a culture of abundance.
