ReDelicious is a food lab co-op transforming food waste into delicious, sustainable, and educational experiences. We host fermentation workshops, community feasts, and mutual aid events throughout DC.

Every Sunday, we gather and build community through cooking, preserving, redistributing, and foraging for delicious food. All are welcome and the food is free.
